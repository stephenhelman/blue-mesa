# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Homeowners in El Paso and the surrounding 915 area considering a custom pool or
spa. They are evaluating a significant, high-trust home investment and are often
browsing on a phone. Their job: quickly decide this is a credible, premium local
builder, and reach out (call/text or book a free in-home consultation) with low
friction.

## Product Purpose
A mobile-first marketing site that makes Blue Mesa Pools & Spas feel like a
premium local craftsman and captures leads. Two conversions, in priority order:
(1) phone call / text to +1 (915) 229-1558; (2) free in-home consultation form
submission (routes to Google Sheets now, GoHighLevel later via a swappable sink).
Success = the site looks elite on a phone and reliably captures leads.

## Brand Personality
"Building Backyard Luxury." Premium, grounded, local craftsman. Confident and
calm, not loud or salesy. The brand mark is a desert mesa with a rising sun over
water: navy + pool blue, with a single warm gold sun accent. The feeling target
is luxury through restraint, whitespace, and craft, not clip-art or coupon energy.

## Anti-references
- Big-box / franchise pool sellers (generic, transactional, stocky).
- Default Tailwind / template landing pages.
- Coupon-flyer aesthetics (dashed borders, scissors, shouting discounts).
- Editorial-magazine affectation (display-serif + italic drop caps) — wrong lane
  for a builder.
- AI tells: purple gradients, gradient text, eyebrow-on-every-section, fake
  hero-metric stats, identical card grids.

## Design Principles
- **Trust is the product.** Every choice should read as "a real, capable local
  builder," because the visitor is risking a large purchase.
- **The phone is the storefront.** Mobile-first; tap-to-call is always one thumb
  away.
- **Restraint reads as premium.** Whitespace, one accent, intentional type. Let
  the water and the mesa carry the brand.
- **Make reaching out effortless.** Lead capture and call CTAs are never more
  than a glance away; the form never loses input and always offers a phone
  fallback.
- **Honest placeholders.** Until real project photography arrives, treat imagery
  as deliberately on-brand "coming soon," never as mismatched stock.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA contrast for text and CTAs. Visible, brand-tinted focus states. Full
`prefers-reduced-motion` support (all reveals/parallax collapse to static).
Semantic landmarks and labeled form fields. Touch targets sized for thumbs.
Light theme locked (a pool brand reads bright and airy).
